The product architecture

Eight Universes. One life, finally organized.

Most apps hand you a feed and call it a life. 4everacy organizes your whole life into eight distinct Universes across two orbital rings — each a self-contained dimension with its own intelligence, its own data, and its own quiet kind of power. Every Universe speaks to the others. None of them harvests you.
